FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to buy any hardware to use AgroFlow? ▼
No hardware is required to get started. AgroFlow works with publicly available satellite data and weather APIs from day one. If you already have soil moisture sensors, you can integrate them via our open API for even more precision. Hardware is optional, not a prerequisite.
How long does the free trial last? ▼
All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to every feature in that tier. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you choose whether to subscribe or not — no automatic charges.
What crops does AgroFlow support? ▼
AgroFlow currently supports over 120 crop varieties including wheat, corn, tomato, olive, citrus, grape, rice, potato, soybean, and more. Our crop library is continuously expanding — if your crop isn't listed, contact us and we'll add it.
Can I manage multiple farms under one account? ▼
Yes. The Harvest (Pro) plan supports up to 25 fields and 5 users per farm. For larger organizations managing multiple distinct farm locations, the Agribusiness (Enterprise) plan offers unlimited fields and multi-farm management with role-based access control.
How accurate are AgroFlow's recommendations? ▼
AgroFlow combines four independent data sources — soil sensors, weather forecasts, satellite imagery, and crop models — to generate recommendations with over 90% accuracy across our pilot farms. Accuracy improves over time as the AI learns the specific conditions of your fields.
Can I cancel at any time? ▼
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts and no cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you're on an annual plan, you'll retain access until the end of your paid period.
